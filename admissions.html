Admissions

The enrollment process at PTC depends on where you're from. Choose your path below and we'll walk you through every step.

🇺🇸

Domestic Students

US citizens and permanent residents. Straightforward enrollment — no visa required, no TSA security clearance. Choose Part 141 or Part 61 and start flying.

  • US citizens and green card holders
  • Part 141 or Part 61 — your choice
  • No visa or TSA screening required
  • FAA Medical Certificate required
  • Start in as little as 1–2 weeks
Domestic Enrollment Guide →
🌍

International Students

Non-US citizens coming from outside the United States. Requires M-1 student visa, TSA/FTSP clearance, and an I-20 from PTC. Plan for a 2–4 month process before arriving.

  • All non-US citizens and non-residents
  • Part 141 only (required for M-1 visa)
  • M-1 student visa + TSA/FTSP clearance required
  • I-20 issued by PTC upon acceptance
  • Plan 2–4 months before arriving in the US

At a Glance

Domestic vs. International

Not sure which path applies to you? Here's a quick comparison.

🇺🇸 Domestic 🌍 International
Visa Required ✗ No ✓ M-1 Visa
TSA / FTSP Clearance ✗ No ✓ Required
Available Programs Part 141 or Part 61 Part 141 Only
Pre-Training Fees ~$75–$150 (medical) ~$990–$1,265
Time to Start Training 1–2 weeks 2–4 months
FAA Medical Required ✓ Yes (both) ✓ Yes (both)
