About PTC

Born from a passion for aviation and a belief that the sky should be accessible to everyone — locally and globally.

Who We Are

More Than a Flight School

PTC — Pilot Training Center was founded on a simple idea: that learning to fly should be structured, affordable, and available to anyone with the drive to pursue it — whether you're from La Verne, California or from halfway around the world.

We operate out of Brackett Field Airport (POC) in La Verne, California — one of Southern California's most active general aviation airports, surrounded by the San Gabriel Valley foothills and minutes from Los Angeles.

Our fleet of six Cessna 172N Skyhawks and our team of dedicated, FAA-certified instructors have guided hundreds of students — domestic and international — from their very first flight to their FAA Commercial Pilot Certificate.

Our Home

Brackett Field Airport, La Verne

Brackett Field (FAA: POC) sits at the foot of the San Gabriel Mountains in La Verne, California — a quiet, safe city 30 miles east of Los Angeles. With consistently clear skies, varied terrain, and access to some of the most complex and rewarding airspace in the country, it's one of the best places in the US to learn to fly.

Training at Brackett means you'll fly over the mountains, the valleys, the Pacific coastline, and the sprawling LA basin — building skills and confidence that pilots at quieter airports simply don't get.
