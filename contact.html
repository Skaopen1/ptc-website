Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a PPL?

Most students complete their Private Pilot License in 3–5 months training 2–3 days per week.

Do you offer financing or payment plans?

Contact us to discuss payment options. Many students fund training through personal savings, loans, or employer tuition assistance.

Can I train if I wear glasses?

Yes! Many pilots wear corrective lenses. Your FAA Medical will confirm your eligibility. Your glasses must meet FAA visual acuity standards.

What if I need to pause training?

Life happens. Part 61 is especially flexible. Just communicate with your instructor — we'll work around your schedule.
