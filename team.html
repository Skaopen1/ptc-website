The People Behind PTC

Meet Our Team

Experienced, passionate, and dedicated — our instructors and staff live and breathe aviation.

👨‍✈️
Khalid A.
Co-Founder

With over 3,500 hours in the Cessna 172 and a passion for aviation education, Khalid founded PTC to bring world-class flight training to the San Gabriel Valley. A Gold Seal CFI and CFII, he has guided students from 14 countries to their FAA certificates.

ATP CFI CFII MEI Gold Seal
✈️
Kareem M.
Chief Flight Instructor

Kareem oversees daily flight operations, fleet maintenance coordination, and student scheduling at PTC. A Commercial Pilot with an Instrument Rating, he ensures every student gets maximum aircraft availability and a seamless training experience.

CPL Instrument Multi-Engine
🎓
Sarah M.
Senior Flight Instructor — CFI / CFII

Sarah is one of PTC's most sought-after instructors, specializing in instrument training and checkride preparation. She brings a methodical, student-first approach to every lesson, with a 98% first-attempt checkride pass rate among her students.

CFI CFII Commercial
🛩️
Marcus T.
Flight Instructor — CFI

A Southern California native and Brackett Airport regular since childhood, Marcus joined PTC after earning his Commercial certificate here as a student. He specializes in training new students from their discovery flight all the way through solo.

CFI Commercial Instrument
🌍
Aisha K.
International Student Coordinator & CFI

Aisha is PTC's dedicated support lead for international students. Fluent in English and Arabic, she guides students through the TSA/FTSP process, I-20 applications, and visa documentation — ensuring a smooth arrival and start to training.

CFI Commercial Bilingual EN/AR
⚙️
James R.
Director of Maintenance & Safety

James keeps PTC's fleet of six Cessna 172Ns in peak airworthy condition. An FAA-certificated Airframe & Powerplant mechanic with 15 years on general aviation aircraft, his standards are uncompromising — because safe training starts with a safe aircraft.

A&P Mechanic IA 15 Yrs Exp.
