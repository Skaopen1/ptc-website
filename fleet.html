Why the 172N

The World's Best Training Aircraft

The Cessna 172 Skyhawk is the most-produced aircraft in history for good reason.

🛡️

Proven Safety

Millions of flight hours worldwide. The 172's forgiving handling and robust design set the safety standard for training aircraft globally.

🔧

Easy to Maintain

Simple, well-understood systems mean faster turnarounds and more time in the air. Every mechanic knows the 172 inside and out.

📡

Classic Avionics

Steam gauges give student pilots a strong foundation in raw piloting skills before transitioning to glass cockpits later in their careers.

🌄

Built for Training

High-wing design gives exceptional visibility. Gentle stall characteristics and stable flight make it ideal for building pilot confidence.
