Our Programs

Choose Your Path to the Skies

Whether you're a weekend flyer or pursuing an aviation career, we have the right program for you.

Part 141

Structured Career Training

FAA-approved syllabus with a defined curriculum. Ideal for dedicated students and international pilots seeking the most efficient route to certification.

  • FAA-approved structured syllabus
  • Accepted for international student visas (TSA/AFSP)
  • Reduced minimum flight hour requirements
  • Private, Instrument & Commercial ratings
  • Stage checks and structured progress reviews
Explore Part 141
Part 61

Flexible & Personalized

Train on your schedule. Part 61 offers maximum flexibility with no fixed syllabus, perfect for local students balancing work, school, or life.

  • Flexible scheduling — fly when you can
  • Personalized to your pace and goals
  • No stage check requirements
  • Private, Instrument & Commercial ratings
  • Great for recreational & career pilots alike
